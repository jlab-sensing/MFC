# Instructions for Construction of Soil MFC Prototypes
## _June 2022_
### Jack Baskin Engineering, The University of California, Santa Cruz
This document is an instruction manual on how to re-create the soil microbial fuel cells constructed by Colleen Josephson's labratory at The University of California, Santa Cruz. Please note, this document is an instruction on the physical construction of the fuel cells only. Code and circuit documentation can be found elsewhere in the git repository.
###### Required Materials
- Large Plastic Bin *get dimensions*
- Soil
- Unchlorintated water
- Burlap
- Carbon Felt
- Wire
